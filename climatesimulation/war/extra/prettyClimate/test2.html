Description: This is a climate simulation that allows you to see how the heat could distribute given increased carbon levels in the atmosphere. Increasing the milliseconds per emission is the approximation of adding more carbon in the atmosphere.
The central idea I had when making this is that the parts of the earth that are hot should increase very unevenly given a reduction in emissions. The physics simulation shows that.
